# Coding Agents

## Summary

Coding agents were the conference's central object, but not as a single product category. Across talks they appeared as terminal workers, remote sessions, IDE copilots, SDLC participants, PR producers, and future infrastructure tenants. The useful synthesis is that coding agents are no longer only assistants inside a chat box; they are becoming actors in software delivery systems.

## Why It Matters

This topic matters outside the conference because it is one of the pressure points teams hit when they move from AI demos to AI engineering practice. It affects architecture, team workflow, product design, evaluation, or deployment reliability.
