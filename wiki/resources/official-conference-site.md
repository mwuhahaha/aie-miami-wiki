# Official Conference Site

## What It Is

The official AI Engineer Miami page provides conference dates, location, framing, speaker roster, schedule, venue, and sponsor context.

## Why It Matters

This resource explains what material is allowed to support AIE Wiki pages. It is intentionally public and tied only to the allowed source boundary.

## How It Is Used

Pages use this resource only as evidence for source labeling and provenance. Transcript files support what was said on stage; the official site supports roster, schedule, venue, and official framing; the source-boundary note explains what the wiki intentionally excludes.
