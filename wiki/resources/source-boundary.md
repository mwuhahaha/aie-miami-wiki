# Source Boundary

## What It Is

The public wiki is intentionally restricted to the two transcripts, the public conference website, and clearly labeled public-web supporting sources.

## Why It Matters

This resource explains what material is allowed to support AIE Wiki pages. It is intentionally public and excludes private local projects, recordings, diary projects, queue state, prior generated pages, and mixed-vault content.

## How It Is Used

Pages use this resource only as evidence for source labeling and provenance. Transcript files support what was said on stage; the official site supports roster, schedule, venue, and official framing; the source-boundary note explains what the wiki intentionally excludes.
